Princess to The Rescue

Construct 2 game, with graphics made in Photoshop and Illustrator

This once happy and prosperous kingdom has been put under a horrible curse, and it's up to you to save the people and restore peace to the realm! Princess to the Rescue is a puzzle platformer created for young children, where they can be the heroine of the story and save the day.
