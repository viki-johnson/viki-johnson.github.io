About Me

I have been working in Unity for over five years, starting as an AR and VR programmer and designer, working on amazing projects with clients such as the National Gallery, Aardman and Simple Things Music Festival.

During these projects, I learnt how to make shaders and slowly transitioned into a technical artist, which is a great way of combining my art skills and programming knowledge. And now I am a technical artist full time!

In my spare time, I make little video games (taking part in lots of game jams), and love drawing and creating digital art.

If you would like to contact me please email: viki@alittleredpanda.com
