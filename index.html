About Me

I work as a programmer, building augmented reality software in the south west of England. In my spare time, I make little video games (taking part in lots of game jams), and love drawing and creating digital art.

I build AR software with: AR Kit & Unity, Spark AR Studio
I make games in: Unity, Construct 2, Bitsy
I code art with: CSS, SVGs, Processing, Unity Shaders
I draw and paint with: Adobe Photoshop, Abobe Illustrator, Clip Paint Studio
